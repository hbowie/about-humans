50. We tend to form preferences for particular structures.

All of the various organizing structures have their uses, but people often form strong preferences for some structures over others.

For example those with strong egalitarian leanings may favor multicultural societies.

Others are much more comfortable with a hierarchical structure.

However modern human society needs all of these different structures in order to function well, so we humans are better off learning some flexibility around these things.
