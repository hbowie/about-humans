16. We invent stuff.

We humans design and build things that never before existed. We figure things out, and apply that knowledge to creating things that operate in new ways, and perform new functions in aid of humanity.

Words from Others on this Topic

Beavers build houses; but they build them in nowise differently, or better, now than they did five thousand years ago…. Man is not the only animal who labors; but he is the only one who improves his workmanship. These improvements he effects by Discoveries and Inventions.

Abraham Lincoln, 1858, from the Lecture “Lecture on Discoveries and Inventions”

Don’t worry about what anybody else is going to do…. The best way to predict the future is to invent it. Really smart people with reasonable funding can do just about anything that doesn’t violate too many of Newton’s Laws.

Alan Kay, 1971, from the Remarks “Meeting of the Palo Alto Research Center”
