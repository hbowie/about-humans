3. We strive for balance between competing concerns and perspectives.

Humans have the ability to see the same issue from multiple perspectives, and to weigh competing concerns against one another, in order to arrive at optimal solutions and conclusions.

This sort of continual balancing act is part of what makes us such complex beasts.
