III. The Restless Animal  ↑

16. We humans have powerful imaginations.

We can imagine possible futures, and can imagine things that do not yet exist.

Note that our imagining is strongly reliant on our language abilities.

Words from Others on this Topic

You see things; and you say, ‘Why?’ But I dream things that never were; and I say, ‘Why not?’

George Bernard Shaw, 1922, from the play Back to Methuselah
