4. Culture acts as the operating system for a group.

Humans are flexible enough to operate in many different kinds of groups, for a variety of purposes.

A culture provides a sort of operating system for a group.

Culture includes knowledge, customs, languages, technology and artifacts that are shared between group members, and passed on from one generation of members to the next.
