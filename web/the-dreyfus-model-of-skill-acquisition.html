5. The Dreyfus model of skill acquisition

Link: Wikipedia: Dreyfus model of skill acquisition

This model describes how learners typically proceed through five general phases as part of a complete education in a new skill.

This model describes the learning journey as one starting with complete dependence on cultural prescription, and then proceeding to ever-greater levels of individual autonomy, providing the practitioner with increasing freedom to adapt general rules to the specifics of a situation.
